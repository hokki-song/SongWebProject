Woman is the Future of Man (2004)

Hahaha (2010)
Hong has received the Prix Un Certain Regard at the 2010 Cannes Film Festival
Our Sunhi (2013)
the Silver Leopard Award for Best Director at the 2013 Locarno International Film Festival
Right Now, Wrong Then (2015)
the Golden Leopard at the 2015 Locarno International Film Festival
